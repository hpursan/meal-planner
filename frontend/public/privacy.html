Last updated: December 21, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is Meal Planner's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, generate a meal plan, or contact support.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Meal Planner service:

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Settings" menu in the app.
